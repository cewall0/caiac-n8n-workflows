# Sunshine Ridge HOA — Community Rules & Enforcement

## General Conduct

All residents and their guests are expected to maintain a respectful, peaceful environment throughout Sunshine Ridge. These rules apply to all common areas, shared amenities, and the exterior of individual units.

**Quiet Hours:** 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM Sunday through Thursday. 11:00 PM to 9:00 AM Friday and Saturday. During quiet hours, noise from music, gatherings, power tools, or other sources must not be audible from neighboring units or common areas.

**Common Area Use:** Common areas including the clubhouse lawn, walking paths, and mailbox areas are available to residents and their guests only. Commercial solicitation in common areas is prohibited. Residents are responsible for cleaning up after themselves and their guests.

**Smoking:** Smoking and vaping are prohibited in all common areas, including pool decks, the fitness center, and the clubhouse. Smoking is permitted on private patios and balconies only, provided it does not create a nuisance for neighbors.

## Trash & Recycling

Trash must be placed in the designated bins inside the trash enclosure — never left outside or beside the enclosure. Recycling bins are provided for paper, cardboard, glass, and plastic. Large item disposal (furniture, appliances) must be scheduled through the management office by calling (317) 555-0142. Unauthorized bulk items left in the trash area will be removed at the resident's expense ($75 fee).

Trash collection is every Tuesday and Friday. Recycling is collected every other Thursday (schedule posted in the clubhouse and on the community portal).

## Pets

Dogs and cats are permitted (maximum 2 pets per unit). Pets must be on a leash at all times in common areas. Pet waste must be picked up immediately using the bags provided at stations throughout the community. Residents are responsible for any damage or injury caused by their pets.

Restricted breeds include: American Pit Bull Terrier, Rottweiler, Doberman Pinscher, and wolf hybrids. If you have a pet that may fall under a restricted breed, contact the management office for a review before bringing the animal to the community.

## Fine Schedule

Violations are issued in writing via email and first-class mail. Residents have 10 business days to cure a violation before a fine is assessed.

| Violation | First Offense | Second Offense | Third+ Offense |
|---|---|---|---|
| Noise during quiet hours | Warning | $100 | $200 |
| Unauthorized bulk trash | $75 | $150 | $300 |
| Pet off-leash | $50 | $100 | $200 |
| Pet waste not picked up | $75 | $150 | $300 |
| Parking violation | $50 | $100 | $200 |
| Unapproved exterior modification | $250 | $500 | Board review |

## Violation Appeals

Residents may appeal any fine within 15 business days of the notice date. To appeal, submit a written request to the management office at manager@sunshineridgehoa.org or by mail to: Sunshine Ridge HOA, 4800 Ridgeline Drive, Suite 100, Carmel, IN 46033.

Include your unit number, the violation date, fine amount, and a brief explanation. The board reviews appeals at the monthly meeting (typically the second Tuesday of each month). You will be notified of the outcome within 5 business days of the meeting. Attending the meeting in person to speak to your appeal is permitted — request time on the agenda when you submit your appeal.

Fines that are not paid and not under active appeal are subject to a 1.5% monthly late fee and may be referred to a collections attorney. Unpaid balances may result in a lien on the property.

## HOA Dues

Monthly HOA dues are $285 per unit, due on the 1st of each month. A $25 late fee applies after the 10th. Dues are collected via the resident portal at sunshineridgehoa.org or by check made payable to Sunshine Ridge HOA. Do not mail cash.

Annual dues increases, if any, are announced no later than November 1 for the following calendar year.
