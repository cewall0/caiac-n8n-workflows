# Sunshine Ridge HOA — Amenities Guide

## Swimming Pool

The Sunshine Ridge pool is located at the northeast corner of the community, adjacent to the clubhouse.

**Season:** Memorial Day weekend through Labor Day weekend (late May through early September).

**Hours:**
- Sunday–Thursday: 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- Friday–Saturday: 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM

**Guest Policy:** Each unit may bring up to 2 guests per visit. Guests must be accompanied by a resident at all times. Children under 14 must be supervised by an adult (18+) at all times. Residents are responsible for their guests' behavior.

**Pool Rules:**
- No glass containers on the pool deck
- No diving (the pool is a uniform 4.5 ft depth)
- No running on the pool deck
- Shower before entering the pool
- Proper swimwear required — cut-off jeans and street clothes are not permitted in the pool
- No pets on the pool deck
- Flotation devices and pool toys permitted for children; large inflatables that obstruct other swimmers are not allowed

**Pool Closure:** The pool may be temporarily closed for maintenance, water quality testing, or inclement weather. Check the community portal or the notice board at the pool entrance for closure announcements.

**Pool Key:** All residents receive a pool key fob at move-in. Replacement fobs are $25, available at the management office during business hours.

## Fitness Center

The fitness center is located on the ground floor of the clubhouse building, accessible via key fob 24 hours a day, 7 days a year.

**Equipment:** Two treadmills, one elliptical, one stationary bike, free weights (5–50 lbs), an adjustable bench, a cable machine, and a resistance band station.

**Rules:**
- Wipe down equipment after each use (wipes are provided)
- Re-rack weights after use
- No personal training or group fitness classes without management approval
- Appropriate athletic attire and closed-toe shoes required
- Maximum occupancy: 10 people

Guests are not permitted in the fitness center.

## Clubhouse

The Sunshine Ridge Clubhouse seats up to 75 people and is available for private resident events.

**Reservation Process:**
1. Check availability on the community portal (sunshineridgehoa.org/amenities)
2. Submit a reservation request at least 7 days in advance
3. Pay the $75 refundable deposit to hold the date
4. Receive confirmation from the management office within 2 business days

**Rental Rates:** $50 for up to 4 hours; $85 for a full day (8 AM–10 PM). The deposit is returned within 5 business days after the event, provided no damage occurred and the space was left clean.

**What's Included:** Tables (round 60" and rectangle 8'), folding chairs (up to 75), kitchenette with refrigerator and microwave, Bluetooth speaker system, and HDMI-connected TV (55").

**Rules:** No smoking inside. No open flames (candles permitted with glass holders). All decorations must be removable without damage. Events must end by 10:00 PM Sunday–Thursday and 11:00 PM Friday–Saturday.

## Walking Paths & Green Spaces

Sunshine Ridge has 1.2 miles of paved walking paths throughout the community. Dogs are welcome on leash. The paths connect to Carmel's Monon Trail at the south entrance.

Two small park areas with benches and picnic tables are available on a first-come, first-served basis. No reservations are needed for the outdoor park areas.

## Package Room

A secure package room is located in Building A lobby, accessible 24/7 with your key fob. You will receive an automated text notification when a package is placed in your assigned locker. Packages unclaimed after 7 days are moved to the management office.
