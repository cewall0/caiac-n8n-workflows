# Sunshine Ridge HOA — Parking Regulations

## Resident Parking

Each unit is assigned one covered parking space in the attached garage structure. Space assignments are listed in your lease or purchase agreement. Do not park in another resident's assigned space — violators will be towed at their expense.

Units may purchase a second parking space if one is available in the surface lot adjacent to Building C. Current rate is $60/month. Contact the management office to join the waitlist (approximately 6–8 month wait as of 2024).

**Vehicle Registration:** All vehicles regularly parked at Sunshine Ridge must be registered with the HOA. Submit your license plate number and vehicle description via the resident portal or at the management office. Unregistered vehicles in assigned spaces are subject to a $50 fine.

## Visitor Parking

Visitor parking spaces are marked with blue signage throughout the community. There are 34 designated visitor spaces across the property.

**Time Limit:** Visitors may park for up to 48 consecutive hours. Vehicles remaining beyond 48 hours without prior approval will be tagged and towed.

**Requesting an Extension:** If you have a guest staying longer than 48 hours (e.g., family visiting for a week), contact the management office in advance by email at manager@sunshineridgehoa.org or by phone at (317) 555-0142. Extensions up to 7 days are typically approved. Extensions beyond 7 days require a written request reviewed by the board.

**No Resident Vehicles in Visitor Spaces:** Resident vehicles may not use visitor parking spaces, even temporarily. This includes moving trucks — contact the management office to arrange temporary access.

## RV, Boat, and Trailer Storage

Recreational vehicles, boats, trailers, and commercial vehicles may not be stored in regular parking spaces or on community property without a storage permit.

**Permit Application:** Submit the RV/Boat/Trailer Storage Permit application (Form HOA-12, available on the portal) with a photo of the vehicle. Permits are issued for designated storage areas only, subject to availability.

**Storage Rates:** $45/month for spaces in the rear storage lot (30 spaces total; current wait time approximately 3–4 months).

Vehicles stored without a permit will be towed within 24 hours of notice.

## Moving Trucks & Large Vehicles

Moving trucks and large delivery vehicles must use the designated loading area at the south entrance of Building B. Do not block fire lanes or the main parking lot entrance.

Contact the management office at least 48 hours in advance to schedule a move-in or move-out so we can ensure elevator access and coordinate the loading area. Move-ins and move-outs are permitted Monday–Saturday, 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM. No moves on Sundays or holidays.

## Towing Policy

Sunshine Ridge partners with Meridian Towing for enforcement. Vehicles may be towed without further notice for:
- Parking in a fire lane
- Blocking a garage exit
- Parking in an unassigned space after one written warning
- Parking in visitor spaces beyond the 48-hour limit
- Unregistered vehicles after 14 days of notice

To retrieve a towed vehicle, contact Meridian Towing at (317) 555-0188. Towing and storage fees are charged by the towing company directly.

## Abandoned Vehicles

A vehicle is considered abandoned if it has not moved in 30 days, has expired tags, or is inoperable. Residents will be given written notice and 10 days to remove the vehicle before it is towed at their expense.

## Electric Vehicle Charging

Four EV charging stations are available in the Building A garage (Spaces A-41 through A-44). Charging is $0.18/kWh, billed to your account via the ChargePoint app. Sessions are limited to 4 hours per vehicle to ensure availability for all residents.
