# Sunshine Ridge HOA — Architectural Review Committee (ARC)

## Purpose

The Architectural Review Committee (ARC) exists to maintain the visual consistency, property values, and community aesthetic of Sunshine Ridge. Any exterior modification to your unit or private outdoor space requires ARC approval before work begins. Starting a project without approval may result in a fine and a mandatory restoration order at your expense.

## What Requires ARC Approval

The following changes require a completed ARC application:

**Always Required:**
- Fences, privacy screens, or trellises of any kind
- Decks, patios, pergolas, and awnings
- Storm doors and screen doors (if different from community standard)
- Window replacements (if changing size, style, or frame color)
- HVAC unit additions or replacements visible from the exterior
- Satellite dishes larger than 18" in diameter
- Solar panels
- Exterior paint color changes (to body, trim, front door, or shutters)
- Additions or structural changes
- Driveways, walkways, or landscaping changes in the front yard or visible side yard

**Not Required:**
- Interior renovations with no exterior visibility
- Like-for-like appliance replacements not visible from outside
- Seasonal decorations (see holiday decoration guidelines)
- Potted plants and container gardens on patios

## The Application Process

1. **Download Form ARC-1** from the resident portal at sunshineridgehoa.org/arc or pick up a copy at the management office.
2. **Complete all sections:** project description, materials, dimensions, colors (include paint chips or material samples if applicable), and contractor information if using one.
3. **Submit with supporting documents:** site plan or diagram showing placement, photos of the current area, and product/material specifications.
4. **Submit to:** manager@sunshineridgehoa.org (email preferred) or drop off at the management office.

**Review Timeline:** The ARC has 30 calendar days to approve, deny, or request additional information. If you have not heard back within 30 days, contact the management office — do not assume approval.

**Expedited Review:** For time-sensitive projects, request expedited review (Form ARC-1E). A $50 fee applies. Expedited decisions are typically made within 10 business days.

## Approved Paint Colors

Sunshine Ridge uses a curated palette from Sherwin-Williams. Approved colors are listed on the community portal under "ARC Resources." Front door accent colors have 14 approved options. If you wish to use a color not on the approved list, submit a variance request with a sample; the ARC will review at the next monthly meeting.

## Fences

Fences are permitted only in backyard and side-yard areas not visible from the street or common areas. Maximum height is 6 feet. Approved materials are wood (stained or painted in an approved color) and black powder-coated aluminum. Chain link, vinyl, and split rail fences are not approved at Sunshine Ridge.

## Solar Panels

Solar panel installations are permitted provided they are:
- Not visible from the street (rear or non-street-facing roof slopes preferred)
- Installed by a licensed contractor
- Compliant with all local building codes and permits
- Submitted with a diagram showing panel placement

The ARC may not deny a solar installation that complies with Indiana state law regarding solar access rights, but may request aesthetic accommodations where feasible.

## Violations & Restoration Orders

If you make an exterior modification without ARC approval, you will receive a violation notice. The fine is $250 for first offense, $500 for second. Additionally, the ARC may require you to restore the property to its original condition within 60 days. If restoration is not completed, the HOA may arrange restoration and bill the cost to the homeowner.
