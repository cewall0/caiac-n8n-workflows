# Sunshine Ridge HOA — Move-In/Out & Maintenance Requests

## Move-In Checklist

Welcome to Sunshine Ridge! Before your first day, please complete the following:

1. **Register your vehicles** — Submit license plates via the resident portal or at the management office. You'll need this done before using your assigned parking space.
2. **Pick up your key fobs** — The management office issues 2 key fobs per unit at no charge. Additional fobs are $25 each. You'll need your fob for pool access, fitness center, package room, and building entry.
3. **Set up your portal account** — Go to sunshineridgehoa.org and register with your unit number and the access code provided in your welcome packet. The portal is where you'll pay dues, submit maintenance requests, reserve the clubhouse, and check community announcements.
4. **Review your parking assignment** — Your assigned space number is in your welcome packet. If there's a vehicle already in your space, contact the office immediately.
5. **Complete your pet registration** (if applicable) — Submit a photo and description of your pet(s) via the portal or the Pet Registration Form (Form HOA-7).

## Scheduling Your Move

Moves must be scheduled at least 48 hours in advance. To schedule:
- Email: manager@sunshineridgehoa.org with your unit number, preferred date/time, and estimated duration
- Phone: (317) 555-0142 (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–5 PM)

**Move-In/Out Hours:** Monday–Saturday, 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM. Sunday moves are not permitted. No moves on major holidays (New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas).

**Elevator Reservation:** For units in Buildings A and B, one elevator will be padded and reserved for your use during your scheduled window. Confirm your elevator reservation when you schedule your move.

**Loading Area:** Use the designated loading area at the south entrance of Building B only. Do not block the main garage entrance, fire lanes, or visitor parking.

**Move-In Deposit:** A $200 refundable elevator/common area deposit is required for all moves. The deposit is returned within 10 business days after your move, pending an inspection of the elevator and common areas for damage.

## Maintenance Requests

For any issue with your unit or a common area, submit a maintenance request through the resident portal at sunshineridgehoa.org/maintenance. You can also email maintenance@sunshineridgehoa.org.

**What the HOA is responsible for:** Common area maintenance (hallways, stairwells, pool, fitness center, landscaping, exterior of buildings, roofs, gutters, and shared mechanical systems such as elevators and building HVAC).

**What the resident is responsible for:** Interior of the unit, including appliances, interior plumbing (from the shutoff valve inward), interior electrical, flooring, windows (glass), and private patio/balcony furniture.

**Response Times:**
- Emergency (water leak, no heat/AC, elevator entrapment): Immediate — call (317) 555-0142 (24-hour emergency line)
- Urgent (pool equipment failure, gate not working): Within 1 business day
- Standard (landscaping concern, exterior light out, common area damage): Within 5 business days

Always include photos with your maintenance request — this speeds up assessment and scheduling significantly.

## Move-Out Procedures

Before vacating your unit, please:
1. Return all key fobs to the management office (or you will be charged $25/fob)
2. Schedule a move-out inspection at least 5 business days in advance
3. Ensure your unit is clean and free of debris before the inspection
4. Remove all personal items from common areas, storage, and parking spaces
5. Provide a forwarding address for your security deposit and any outstanding mail

Outstanding HOA dues, fines, or balances must be settled before or at the time of your move-out. Balances not cleared within 30 days will be sent to collections and may affect your credit.
