# Working With Hargrove Realty Group

## Who We Are

Hargrove Realty Group is an independent real estate agency serving buyers and sellers in the greater metro area. We're a small team that keeps our client list intentional — we'd rather do great work for fewer clients than average work for many.

Our agents have a combined 30+ years of experience across residential sales, investment properties, and relocation. Every client works directly with a licensed agent, not a junior assistant or a call center.

## Buyer Representation

We represent buyers exclusively under a Buyer Agency Agreement. This means we have a fiduciary duty to you — we're legally and ethically required to act in your best interest, not the seller's.

**What buyer representation includes:**
- Access to all MLS listings plus off-market opportunities
- Custom property search alerts — new listings matching your criteria delivered to you the moment they're listed
- Property tours scheduled at your convenience
- Detailed analysis of any home before you make an offer (comparable sales, days on market, price history)
- Offer writing and negotiation strategy
- Coordination of inspection, appraisal, and all third parties
- Transaction management from accepted offer to closing day

**Our fee:** In most transactions, the seller pays the buyer's agent commission. We'll explain the current fee structure at your buyer consultation — the NAR settlement in 2024 brought changes to how buyer agency compensation works, and we'll make sure you understand it before signing anything.

## Seller Representation (Listing)

When we list your home, you get a full-service marketing and transaction management package — not a discount entry in the MLS.

**Our listing service includes:**
- Comparative Market Analysis and pricing strategy session
- Pre-listing preparation walkthrough and recommendations
- Professional photography (included, not an add-on)
- MLS listing with maximum syndication
- Showing coordination through ShowingTime
- Offer review and negotiation support
- Transaction coordination through closing

Our listing commission is competitive with the market. We'll discuss it at the listing consultation.

## How We Communicate

We default to text and email for day-to-day updates — it's faster and creates a record. For negotiations and major decisions, we'll call or meet in person. You can expect a response from us within 2–4 business hours during the week, and same-day on weekends when we're actively working your transaction.

We don't disappear after you sign a contract. We track every milestone and flag anything that needs your attention proactively.

## What We Ask of Our Clients

A real estate transaction moves quickly and requires your participation. To keep things on track:

- Respond to document requests within 24 hours (especially during underwriting)
- Keep your financial situation stable once you're under contract — no new debt, no large deposits, no job changes
- Be available for showings on reasonable notice (sellers) or be ready to move when the right home appears (buyers)
- Trust the process — there will be stressful moments, but most transactions that start solid end at the closing table

## Areas We Serve

We focus on residential sales within a 30-mile radius of downtown. If your property falls outside that area, we'll tell you upfront and refer you to an agent who knows that market well rather than stretch beyond our expertise.

## Getting Started

**Buyers:** Schedule a buyer consultation — 30–45 minutes, no commitment, no pressure. We'll talk through your goals, timeline, and what to expect, and answer every question you have before you decide whether we're the right fit.

**Sellers:** Schedule a listing consultation. We'll tour the property, pull the CMA, and present our marketing plan. You'll leave with a clear picture of your home's value and what it would look like to work with us.

Both consultations are free. Contact us through the website or call/text our main line.
