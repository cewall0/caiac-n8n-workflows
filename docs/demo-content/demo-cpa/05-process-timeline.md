# Greenfield CPA — Our Process & Timeline

## The Tax Preparation Process

Here's exactly what working with Greenfield CPA looks like, from initial contact through your filed return.

---

### Step 1: Initial Consultation (Week 1)

A 30–60 minute meeting (in-person, phone, or video) to understand your situation and confirm we're the right fit. We'll discuss:
- Your filing situation and any significant changes from last year
- Our services and fees for your specific return
- What documents you'll need to gather

There's no charge for the initial consultation. At the end, you'll sign an engagement letter and make a deposit if you'd like to proceed.

**For existing clients:** If your situation is unchanged from last year, we skip the consultation and move directly to document collection.

---

### Step 2: Document Collection (January–March)

Most tax documents arrive in January and early February. We send you a document request checklist (customized to your situation from last year) and access to our secure client portal where you can upload documents directly.

**Our secure portal:** Available at portal.greenfieldcpa.com. Drag-and-drop uploading; we're notified immediately when documents arrive. No emailing sensitive documents.

**When to submit:** We work on returns in the order documents are received. Submit everything together once you have all your documents — submitting piecemeal slows down your return and may result in a queue restart. If you're waiting on one specific document (K-1s often arrive late), let us know so we can give you an estimated timing.

---

### Step 3: Preparation (1–3 Weeks After Document Submission)

Our preparation timeline varies by time of year:
- **January–February submissions:** Returns typically prepared within 5–7 business days
- **March submissions:** 7–10 business days
- **April 1–14 (crunch period):** 3–5 business days for straightforward returns; complex returns may be extended

During preparation, we may contact you with questions. Respond promptly — delays in your response delay your return.

---

### Step 4: Review Call (15–30 Minutes)

Before we finalize your return, we schedule a brief call to walk through:
- Your bottom-line result (refund or balance due)
- Any significant changes from last year
- Notable deductions we applied
- Planning opportunities for next year

For straightforward returns, we may send a summary email rather than scheduling a call — we'll follow your preference.

---

### Step 5: Finalize & e-File

Once you've reviewed and approved your return, we send your e-signature authorization forms through DocuSign. After you sign:
- Federal and state returns are e-filed within 1 business day
- You receive IRS and state e-file confirmation numbers within 24–72 hours of filing

**Paper filing:** Most returns are e-filed. If your return must be paper-filed for any reason (prior-year balance on account, first-year filing, etc.), we'll let you know and mail it for you.

---

### Extensions

If we cannot complete your return by the April 15 deadline (waiting on documents, complex situations, late-arriving K-1s), we file an automatic extension on your behalf. The extension gives you until October 15 to file.

**Important:** An extension extends the filing deadline, not the payment deadline. If you owe taxes, they're still due April 15. We estimate your liability and help you make an extension payment to avoid underpayment penalties.

## Key 2024 Tax Deadlines

| Deadline | What's Due |
|---|---|
| January 31, 2025 | W-2s, 1099-NECs mailed by payers |
| February 15, 2025 | 1099-DIV, 1099-INT, 1099-B mailed by payers |
| March 15, 2025 | S-Corp (1120-S) and Partnership (1065) returns; S-Corp election deadline for 2025 |
| April 15, 2025 | Individual (1040) and C-Corp (1120) returns; Q1 estimated taxes due |
| April 15, 2025 | Last day to contribute to IRA or HSA for 2024 |
| June 16, 2025 | Q2 estimated taxes due |
| September 15, 2025 | Q3 estimated taxes due; extended S-Corp and Partnership returns due |
| October 15, 2025 | Extended individual and C-Corp returns due |
| January 15, 2026 | Q4 2025 estimated taxes due |
