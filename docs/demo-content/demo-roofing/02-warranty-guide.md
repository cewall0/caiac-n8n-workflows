# Apex Roofing — Warranty Guide

## Two Types of Warranties

Every Apex Roofing project comes with two distinct warranties: the manufacturer's product warranty and Apex's own workmanship warranty. Understanding both is important.

---

## Manufacturer's Warranty

Covers defects in the roofing material itself — shingles that crack, curl, or fail before their rated lifespan due to a manufacturing defect.

### Duration by Product
| Material | Manufacturer Warranty |
|---|---|
| 3-tab asphalt shingles | 20–25 years (prorated after year 10) |
| Architectural shingles (standard) | 30 years (prorated after year 10) |
| Architectural shingles (premium lines, e.g. GAF Timberline HDZ) | Lifetime limited |
| Class 4 impact-resistant shingles | Lifetime limited |
| Standing seam metal | 40 years (Kynar paint) / lifetime structural |
| TPO membrane | 15–30 years depending on membrane thickness |

### Important Notes on Manufacturer Warranties
- **"Lifetime" means the life of the home, not forever.** It typically covers the original homeowner; coverage reduces after transfer of ownership (varies by manufacturer).
- **Prorated warranties:** After the non-prorated period, the payout is reduced by the years of use. A shingle in year 20 of a 30-year prorated warranty may only receive 33% of replacement cost.
- **Contractor certification matters:** Manufacturer warranties are only valid when installed by a certified contractor. Apex is a GAF Master Elite Contractor and a CertainTeed SELECT ShingleMaster — certifications that unlock enhanced warranty tiers not available from non-certified roofers.
- **Enhanced warranties:** Apex customers installing qualifying GAF products are eligible for the GAF System Plus Warranty (50-year non-prorated coverage on materials + 10-year labor) at no additional charge — a significant upgrade over the standard warranty.

## Apex Workmanship Warranty

Our workmanship warranty covers the quality of Apex's installation — if the roof leaks or fails due to an error in our installation, we fix it at no charge.

**Duration:** 10 years on all Apex-installed roofs

**What it covers:**
- Leaks caused by improper flashing installation (chimneys, vents, valleys, skylights)
- Improper nail placement or fastening patterns
- Improperly sealed penetrations
- Any defect attributable to installation error rather than product failure

**What it does NOT cover:**
- Damage from storms, hail, wind, ice dams, or other weather events (this is an insurance claim)
- Normal wear and tear beyond the material's rated lifespan
- Damage caused by other trades working on the roof after our installation (HVAC, solar installers)
- Damage from foot traffic by non-Apex personnel

**Transferable:** The Apex workmanship warranty is transferable to a subsequent homeowner one time within the 10-year window. Provide us with the new owner's name and contact information at the time of sale and we'll update the warranty record.

## Storm Damage and Your Warranty

Storm damage (hail, wind) is covered by your homeowners insurance — not your roofing warranty. The warranty covers product defects and installation errors, not acts of nature.

After a hail storm, we recommend:
1. Call Apex for a free damage inspection (we'll document everything)
2. We provide a written damage assessment you can submit to your insurer
3. We work directly with insurance adjusters on your behalf — no need to navigate this alone
4. If the adjuster approves the claim, we coordinate repairs with minimal disruption to you

See our Storm Damage & Insurance Claims Guide for the full process.

## What Voids Your Warranty

To keep your warranty valid:
- Do not allow other contractors (HVAC, solar, satellite dish installers) to walk on or penetrate the roof without notifying Apex first
- Do not use pressure washers on your roof
- Do not apply roof coatings, sealants, or patching materials without consulting us
- Address small repairs promptly — ignored minor damage can void warranty coverage for subsequent related damage
- Keep gutters and downspouts clear — backed-up water can cause damage that is not covered
