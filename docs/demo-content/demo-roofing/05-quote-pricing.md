## What Affects Your Price

Roof replacement cost varies significantly based on these factors:

### 1. Size
Measured in "squares" (100 sq ft each). A 2,000 sq ft home with a moderate-pitch roof might have 20–28 squares of roofing surface — pitch and overhangs add to the total.

### 2. Pitch (Steepness)
- **Low-slope (2:12–4:12):** Easier to walk, lower labor cost
- **Standard (4:12–7:12):** Most common Indiana residential pitch; standard pricing
- **Steep (7:12–12:12):** Requires harnesses and additional crew time; adds $0.50–$1.50/sq ft to labor
- **Very steep (12:12+):** Significant labor premium; specialized safety equipment

### 3. Number of Existing Layers
Indiana building code allows a maximum of 2 layers of asphalt shingles. If your home has 2 existing layers, both must be torn off before new installation. Tear-off labor + disposal typically adds $1.00–$2.00/sq ft to the total.

### 4. Decking Condition
If we find rotted, delaminated, or structurally compromised plywood decking during tear-off, it must be replaced. We price this at $85–$110 per sheet (standard 4×8 sheet of 7/16" OSB, installed). We communicate every decking repair before proceeding.

### 5. Complexity
Every additional element adds cost:
- **Valleys:** Each valley adds flashing material and time
- **Chimneys:** Step and counter flashing + potential masonry work
- **Skylights:** Flashing replacement is critical; improperly flashed skylights are a leading source of leaks
- **Dormers, hips, and multiple roof sections:** Add labor time

### 6. Material Choice
See our Materials Guide for full breakdowns. As a quick reference:

| Material | Installed Cost Range |
|---|---|
| 3-tab asphalt | $3.50–$5.00/sq ft |
| Architectural asphalt (standard) | $4.50–$7.00/sq ft |
| Class 4 impact-resistant architectural | $6.00–$9.00/sq ft |
| Standing seam metal | $10.00–$18.00/sq ft |
| TPO (flat/low-slope) | $5.00–$8.00/sq ft |

## Financing Options

We understand that a $15,000–$25,000 roof replacement is a significant expense. Apex offers financing through our lending partner with the following options:

- **12-month same-as-cash:** No interest if paid in full within 12 months. Minimum payment required monthly. Great for homeowners who want to pay it off quickly or are waiting on an insurance payout.
- **60-month fixed:** Fixed monthly payment over 5 years. Rate depends on credit approval (7–18% APR). Typical $15,000 project = $290–$380/month.
- **120-month fixed:** Extended term for budget-conscious homeowners. Rate depends on credit approval. Typical $15,000 project = $175–$240/month.

Financing applications take approximately 5 minutes and provide an instant decision. Ask your estimator for details.
