# Magnolia Estate — Catering & Bar Policies

## Catering

Magnolia Estate works exclusively with our approved caterer list. Outside caterers not on this list are not permitted. This ensures our couples work with professionals who know our venue, our kitchen layout, and our setup/breakdown expectations.

**Approved Caterers:** Our current list of 12 approved caterers covers a range of styles — plated dinners, family-style, stations, cocktail-only, and various cuisines. The full list with contact information is available in your welcome packet and on the venue portal. When you book, we'll introduce you to three caterers that best match your budget and style.

**Outside Caterer Fee:** If you have a caterer you'd like to use who is not on our approved list, you may apply for a Vendor Approval Exception. A $400 approval fee applies. The caterer must provide proof of liability insurance ($1M minimum), a valid catering license, and agree to our venue rules. We approve approximately 60% of exception requests. The exception process takes 2–3 weeks.

**Food Trucks:** One food truck may be added as a late-night snack station. The food truck must be on our approved vendor list or go through the exception process. Food trucks must park in the designated vendor area (separate from guest parking) and leave by 10:30 PM.

**Outside Food:** Guests may not bring outside food to the event except for the wedding cake/desserts and a small groom's cake (see Cake Policy below). Potluck-style events are not permitted.

## Bar Service

**Licensed Bartenders Required:** Indiana law and our venue insurance require that all alcohol at Magnolia Estate be served by licensed, TIPS-certified bartenders. You may not have guests, friends, or family members serve alcohol under any circumstances.

**Bar Options:**
1. **Full bar through your caterer** — Most of our approved caterers offer bar service packages ranging from $28–$55/person depending on duration and selections. This is the most common and seamless option.
2. **Couple-provided alcohol, caterer-provided bartenders** — You purchase alcohol wholesale and your caterer provides licensed bartenders. Caterer charges a bar service fee ($400–$700) on top of their regular contract. This option typically saves money for larger guest counts.
3. **Bar-only vendor** — You may hire a licensed bar service company separately from your caterer. They must be on our approved vendor list.

**Alcohol End Time:** Alcohol service must end 30 minutes before the contracted event end time. For most Saturday events, last call is 10:00 PM for an 11:00 PM end.

**Under 21 Policy:** Servers must check ID for any guest who appears to be under 30. Magnolia Estate reserves the right to revoke bar service if our staff observes underage drinking.

**Dry Weddings:** Alcohol is optional. A non-alcoholic event requires no special approval and does not change your pricing.

## Cake & Desserts

Your wedding cake and/or dessert table may come from any licensed bakery — it does not need to be on our approved vendor list. Desserts must arrive on the event day (no day-before drop-offs) and be picked up or disposed of at the end of the event.

**Cake Cutting:** If your caterer is handling dessert service, cake cutting is typically included. If your caterer is only doing dinner service (not dessert), a cake cutting fee of $3.50/person applies, charged by Magnolia Estate.

**Dessert Tables:** Space for a dessert table (up to 8' rectangle) is included at no additional charge. Coordinate placement with your venue coordinator.

**Outside Alcohol:** No alcohol may be brought to the venue by the couple, wedding party, or guests. All alcohol must be sourced through the bar service arrangement above. This is a firm policy enforced by our staff.

## Vendor Meals

Your caterer is required to provide hot meals for all vendors working during dinner service (photographer, videographer, DJ/band, coordinator, second shooter, etc.). Vendor meals should be at or near the same time as guest dinner. Vendors eating in the kitchen or vendor area is standard at Magnolia Estate.
