# Magnolia Estate — Cancellation Policy, Weather Plan & FAQs

## Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change and unforeseen circumstances arise. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while protecting both parties.

| Notice Given Before Event | Refund |
|---|---|
| More than 365 days | Full refund less $500 administrative fee |
| 180–365 days | 50% of all payments made (booking deposit non-refundable) |
| 90–180 days | 25% of all payments made |
| Less than 90 days | No refund |

**Booking deposit ($1,500)** is non-refundable in all cancellation scenarios — it holds your date off the market.

**Date Changes:** You may change your event date once at no additional fee, provided the new date is available and the change is made at least 180 days from the original event date. Date changes within 180 days are treated as a cancellation and rebooking.

**Force Majeure:** In the event of cancellation due to circumstances completely outside both parties' control (government-mandated restrictions, declared natural disaster), we will work with you to rebook at no penalty. This does not apply to personal illness, travel issues, or vendor failures.

**We recommend wedding insurance** to protect your investment against the unexpected. Policies through companies like WedSafe or Markel start around $200 and can cover venue cancellation, vendor failure, and weather events.

## Weather & Rain Contingency

Indiana weather is unpredictable. We have a clear, proven rain plan so you never have to worry.

**Outdoor Ceremony (Garden Pavilion):**
The pavilion is fully covered with a permanent roof and rated for all weather conditions. Rain does not cancel a pavilion ceremony — you and your guests stay dry. We do move to the Barn if there is lightning or severe weather (winds over 35 mph).

**Full Indoor Backup:**
If weather requires moving the ceremony inside, we transition to the Barn Reception Hall at no additional charge. The Barn can accommodate both ceremony (chairs theater-style) and reception. We pre-position ceremony chairs inside the Barn for any event with more than a 40% precipitation forecast.

**Decision Timeline:**
- We monitor weather 72 hours out.
- The call to move indoors is made the morning of the event (typically by 9:00 AM) after consultation with you.
- We do not wait until the last minute — your vendors will be notified immediately so they can adjust setup plans.

**Tent Option:**
For couples who want outdoor open-air space regardless of weather, we offer tent rental coordination for the main lawn area. Tents range from $1,500 (30×60 frame tent) to $3,500 (40×80 with sidewalls and flooring). Tents must be ordered at least 30 days in advance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can we do a first look on the property?**
Yes — the magnolia garden, the old oak lane, and the barn exterior are all beautiful first-look spots. Coordinate with your photographer and venue coordinator for timing.

**Is the venue exclusive to us on our event day?**
Yes. You have exclusive use of the entire Magnolia Estate property for the duration of your rental. No other events are scheduled on the property the same day.

**Do you allow sparkler exits?**
Yes, sparkler exits are permitted in the main driveway. We provide a water bucket for safe disposal. Sky lanterns, confetti cannons, and glitter are not permitted.

**Can we have a bonfire or firepit?**
A firepit is available October through April as an add-on ($200). Open bonfires are not permitted due to fire code.

**Do you allow pets at the venue?**
With advance approval, pets may attend the ceremony portion only and must be leashed and supervised. Pets are not permitted inside the Barn during the reception.

**Is there a rehearsal included?**
Your rental does not include a rehearsal walk-through. Rehearsal time can be added as a Friday evening rehearsal dinner package ($1,200) or as a standalone 1-hour walk-through ($250), both subject to availability.

**Can guests stay overnight?**
Magnolia Estate does not have overnight accommodations. We can recommend hotels 10–15 minutes away in Zionsville and Carmel that offer group room block rates. Ask your coordinator for our hotel partner list.

**What is the latest the DJ can play music?**
Inside the Barn, music can continue until 30 minutes before your contracted end time (typically 10:30 PM for a Saturday 11:00 PM end). Outdoor music ends by 10:00 PM per county ordinance.

**Are there additional costs I should plan for?**
Beyond the venue rental, most couples budget for: catering ($75–$145/person), bar service ($28–$55/person), florals ($2,500–$8,000), photography ($2,500–$5,500), DJ ($1,500–$2,500), and a day-of coordinator ($1,200–$3,500). We're happy to connect you with vendors across a range of price points.
