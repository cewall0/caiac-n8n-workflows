# Magnolia Estate — Rental Packages & Pricing

## Rental Packages

### The Full Estate — Weekend Package
Our most popular option. Exclusive use of all venue spaces (Barn, Garden Pavilion, Farmhouse, Bridal Suite, Groom's Cottage, and all grounds) for your entire event.

**Friday:** Venue access 10:00 AM – 11:00 PM
**Saturday:** Venue access 10:00 AM – 11:00 PM
**Sunday (day-after brunch option):** Farmhouse access 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM (add-on, see below)

**2025 Pricing:**
- Peak Season (May–October, Saturdays): $8,500
- Peak Season (May–October, Fridays and Sundays): $6,500
- Off-Peak (November–April, any day): $5,500

### The Barn Only — Weekday Package
Exclusive use of the Barn Reception Hall and Garden Pavilion for weekday events (Monday–Thursday).

- **2025 Pricing:** $3,800 (4-hour minimum; $450/additional hour)
- Bridal Suite and Groom's Cottage available as add-ons ($300 each)

### Rehearsal Dinner Package
Exclusive use of the Farmhouse and garden patio for your rehearsal dinner.

- **2025 Pricing:** $1,200 (4-hour block, Friday evening preferred)
- Includes: Farmhouse, patio, tables and chairs for up to 40

## What's Included in Every Rental

All rental packages include:
- All tables (round 60", rectangle 8', high-top cocktail) and Chiavari chairs for contracted guest count
- White or ivory table linens (color upgrades available through our linen vendor at $8–$18/table)
- Setup and breakdown of venue furniture (your caterer and florist handle their own items)
- Venue-provided LED candles and ambient lighting package (Edison strings, chandeliers, perimeter uplights in copper/warm white)
- Parking attendant for events over 100 guests
- Venue coordinator for day-of logistics (does not replace a personal wedding coordinator)
- Prep kitchen access for caterer (warming only — no commercial cooking equipment)
- Sound system with Bluetooth connectivity and 4 wireless microphones
- Wi-Fi access
- Emergency weather plan (covered transition to Barn if ceremony is outdoors)

## Not Included

- Food and beverage (all catering must go through our approved caterer list)
- Florals, décor, photography, videography, DJ/band, officiant
- Ceremony chairs if separate from reception setup (available to rent through us at $2/chair)
- Day-of wedding coordinator (we strongly recommend one — see our vendor list)
- Overnight accommodations (we can recommend local hotels 10–15 min away)
- Cake cutting fee if cake is provided by an outside bakery ($3.50/person)

## Payment & Deposit Schedule

1. **Booking deposit:** $1,500 due at signing — this secures your date (non-refundable)
2. **50% of balance** due 180 days before your event date
3. **Remaining balance** due 60 days before your event date
4. **Damage deposit:** $500 refundable, collected 30 days before the event; returned within 10 business days post-event if no damage

We accept check, ACH transfer, and all major credit cards (3% processing fee on cards). Payment plans between milestones can be arranged — ask your venue coordinator.
