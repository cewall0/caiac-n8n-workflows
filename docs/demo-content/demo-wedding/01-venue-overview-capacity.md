# Magnolia Estate — Venue Overview & Capacity

## About Magnolia Estate

Magnolia Estate is a full-service wedding and private event venue on 12 acres in Zionsville, Indiana. The property features a restored 1920s farmhouse, a 5,000 sq ft climate-controlled barn reception hall, manicured grounds with formal gardens, a covered outdoor ceremony pavilion, and a private bridal suite and groom's cottage. We host approximately 60 events per year and are available for tours by appointment, Tuesday through Sunday.

## Spaces & Capacities

### The Barn Reception Hall
Our flagship space — a fully restored timber-frame barn with original hand-hewn beams, Edison string lights, wide-plank hardwood floors, and panoramic doors that open to the garden terrace.

- **Seated dinner (with dance floor):** 200 guests
- **Seated dinner (without dance floor):** 250 guests
- **Cocktail reception / standing:** 320 guests
- **Theater-style ceremony (chairs only):** 275 guests

Includes: bridal suite access during the event, commercial prep kitchen, 8 high-top cocktail tables, 25 round 60" dining tables, 200 Chiavari chairs, warming kitchen pass-through.

### The Garden Pavilion (Outdoor Ceremony)
A 40' × 60' covered pavilion surrounded by our rose and magnolia gardens. Ideal for outdoor ceremonies with a natural backdrop.

- **Seated ceremony capacity:** 250 guests
- **Open-air cocktail hour:** 300 guests

The pavilion has a permanent wood floor, electrical hookups for music/microphone, and market lighting. In the event of rain, ceremonies can move to the Barn at no additional charge (see Weather & Rain Contingency).

### The Farmhouse
The original 1920s farmhouse is available for intimate gatherings, rehearsal dinners, and day-of preparation.

- **Seated dinner:** 40 guests
- **Cocktail / social:** 60 guests

Includes: fully renovated interior, original hardwood floors, gas fireplace, private patio, and a full prep kitchen.

### Bridal Suite
A dedicated 800 sq ft bridal preparation suite with natural light, vanity mirrors with lighted stations for 8, a private bathroom, mini fridge, and seating lounge. Available from 10:00 AM on your event day.

### Groom's Cottage
A private 400 sq ft cottage with a sitting area, wet bar, TV, and private entrance. Available from noon on your event day.

## Parking

Magnolia Estate has a private, gravel-surface lot with 150 vehicle spaces. Handicap-accessible spaces are available at the main entrance. Shuttle service from an off-site lot is available for events over 200 guests (shuttle coordination is the couple's responsibility; we can provide vendor recommendations).

## Accessibility

The Barn, Farmhouse, and Garden Pavilion are all single-level and fully accessible. The Bridal Suite is located on the second floor of the Farmhouse and is accessible by staircase only (no elevator). We are happy to arrange alternative bridal preparation space on the ground floor upon request.
