# Magnolia Estate — Vendor Requirements & Day-of Timeline

## Approved Vendor List

Magnolia Estate maintains an approved vendor list for caterers, bar service, and tent rental only. For all other vendor categories — photography, videography, DJ, band, florist, officiant, hair and makeup, photobooth, transportation — you are free to choose any licensed professional.

We do maintain a **Preferred Vendor Directory** of professionals who know our venue well and consistently deliver excellent results. This is a recommendation resource, not a restriction. You can find it on the venue portal or request a copy from your coordinator.

## Vendor Coordinator Requirement

For events with 150 or more guests, a licensed day-of wedding coordinator is required. This is separate from the Magnolia Estate venue coordinator. The venue coordinator manages the venue itself; a day-of coordinator manages your event timeline, wedding party, and vendor orchestration.

For events under 150 guests, a coordinator is strongly recommended but not required. Ask us for coordinator referrals if you need them — we work with several we trust at a range of price points ($1,200–$3,500).

## Vendor Access & Setup Timeline

All vendors must check in with the Magnolia Estate venue coordinator upon arrival. No vendor may begin setup without check-in.

**Standard Access Windows:**
- Florist / Décor: Access from 8:00 AM on the event day
- Caterer: Access from 9:00 AM for prep; kitchen turnover confirmed at check-in
- DJ / Band: Access 3 hours before ceremony start for sound check
- Photographer / Videographer: Access whenever the bridal party is getting ready
- Photo Booth: Access 2 hours before event start
- Officiants and ceremony musicians: Access 1 hour before ceremony start

All vendor vehicles must be parked in the vendor lot (marked on the map in your welcome packet) by 30 minutes before guest arrival. The main guest lot must be clear of vendor vehicles before doors open.

## Breakdown & Load-Out

**Venue End Time:** Your contracted event end time is when music stops and guests begin departing. Your vendor load-out window is typically 1.5 hours after the event end time.

- Standard Saturday event ends at 11:00 PM → Vendor load-out by 12:30 AM
- Caterer must have kitchen cleaned and equipment removed by 1:00 AM
- Floral/décor items left overnight will be discarded at 8:00 AM the following morning
- Damage deposit deductions may apply if the venue is left in poor condition

No venue items (tables, chairs, linens, lighting equipment) may be removed by outside vendors. If you believe an item belongs to your vendor, confirm with the venue coordinator before load-out.

## Music & Sound

DJ and band setup and sound check must be completed before guests arrive. Outdoor music (from the Garden Pavilion) must comply with Hamilton County noise ordinances — music must end by 10:00 PM outdoors. Music inside the Barn may continue until your contracted event end time.

Magnolia Estate provides a house sound system as a backup. Coordinate with your venue coordinator if your band or DJ wants to integrate with the house system.

## Photography & Videography

Drones are permitted on the property with advance notice to the venue coordinator and proof of FAA authorization. Drone operation must comply with local ordinances and may not fly over neighboring properties.

We have a list of beautiful photography locations on the property. Your coordinator will walk your photographer through the grounds during your planning meeting.
